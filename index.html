So, about us..

Street Taco and Beer Co. is a fast, casual restaurant known for it's authentic street style tacos and amazing food, along with a great selection of beers.

Enjoy an incredible meal in our spacious dining area, or enjoy the beautiful Tucson weather and Downtown scenery by sitting at our covered patio.

From our Street Dogs to our Street Nachos, we have plenty of grub to satisfy your craving, and plenty of cold drinks on hand to keep a smile on your face. So bring your family, friends, even co-workers, and enjoy authentic food in an authentic atmosphere.
